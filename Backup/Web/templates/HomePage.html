GeoData

Find Your Customers

What is GeoData?

GeoData is a geographic application designed to help managers and marketing teams in locating their customers on different maps. Using statistical and geographic data from open-source databases, GeoData offers a precise indication of customers on the map; different customers groups can be selected based on average income, age group, marital status and more.

The Features

Italian map analysis

Find your customers in the italian map, by specifying the desired parameters such as income and marital status. The framework also provides an indication on the center of such cluster, based on the density of population of all the target towns.

City map analysis

As big cities show an high density of possible customers, a more in-depth analysis of such cities is available, where it is possible to locate customers in different parts of the town based on different factors (i.e. Postcodes, identified nuclei etc).

Movements data

As half of the italian population travels outside of the city of residence for work or study, it is important to detect such movements to more precisely suggest where advertisement can be placed ( or where stores can be placed to atract commuters).

Find competitors

The framework helps finding competitors and similar brands on the map in the interested area, helping managers in locating groups of stores (where new ones can be placed to exploit the Hotelling's law, or where the damand for certain products is not yet satisfied).

The Technology

Using open-source data from Istat (italian statistical institute), MEF (italian ministry of finances) and census data, a clustering algorithm is applied on over 11 million data rows, in order to find significant groups of possible customers. For city map analysis, data from real-estate websites, openstreetmap and cities open-data is used to show distributions and instances of mvoements and stores.
